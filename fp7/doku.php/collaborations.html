Collaborating Projects and Community

The StratusLab project fosters active collaboration with other related projects in the e-Infrastructures and DCI field and beyond, including:

  • Contrail
  • CYFRONET
  • EDGI
  • EGI
  • EMI
  • ERINA+
  • IGE
  • Mantychore
  • SIENA
  • VENUS-C

In many cases the project has formalised these collaborations by signing a Memorandum of Understanding. You can see the Memoranda of Understanding signed by the Stratuslab project here.
