Support

Here are the support structures available to help:

  • email StratusLab: A team within the project will respond to questions concerning the installation and use of the StratusLab distribution. Fix the address before sending the email!
  • Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ): A page with answers to common questions and problems. Please consult this page before sending a support request.
  • User Forum: Join the StratusLab User Forum by sending a blank email to this address. You can also read user forum messages via the Google Groups website.
